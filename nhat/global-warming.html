Part 1

Vocabulary Focus

Review these key terms used to discuss global problems, then test your understanding.

Air Pollution /eər pəˈluːʃn/

Ô nhiễm không khí: The presence in or introduction into the air of a substance which has harmful or poisonous effects.

"Air pollution in the city has reached alarming levels due to heavy traffic."
Overpopulation /ˌəʊvəˌpɒpjuˈleɪʃn/

Bùng nổ dân số: The condition of being populated with excessively large numbers.

"Overpopulation puts a severe strain on a country's natural resources."
Unemployment /ˌʌnɪmˈplɔɪmənt/

Thất nghiệp: The state of not having a job while being available for work.

"The closing of the factory led to widespread unemployment in the town."

Part 2

Pronunciation: Noun vs. Verb Stress

Many two-syllable words in English change their stress depending on whether they are a Noun or a Verb.

The Rule:
  • Noun: Stress the first syllable.
  • Verb: Stress the second syllable.

Noun

First syllable stressed

RE-cord

"She set a new record for the high jump."


IN-crease

"There has been an increase in pollution."

Verb

Second syllable stressed

re-CORD

"Please record this meeting."


in-CREASE

"Temperatures will increase next year."

Reading & Translation

💡 Useful Patterns

There has been an increase in... (Đã có sự gia tăng trong...)

...is a growing problem. (...là một vấn đề ngày càng tăng.)

The government needs to... (Chính phủ cần phải...)


1. Đã có sự gia tăng trong tình trạng thất nghiệp năm nay.

Air pollution is a growing problem in large cities.

3. Chính phủ cần xây dựng thêm nhiều nhà ở.

The government needs to build more housing.

Part 5

Guided Speaking

Prepare a short speech using the P.E.R. Framework.

The P.E.R. Framework

  • P - Problem: Describe the issue clearly.
  • E - Effect: Explain the negative consequences.
  • R - Resolution: Suggest a practical solution.

Sample Speech: Air Pollution

[Problem] One of the biggest issues in my city is air pollution, caused mostly by vehicle emissions.

[Effect] This leads to serious health problems like asthma, and damages the environment.

[Resolution] The government needs to improve public transport to reduce the number of cars on the road.
